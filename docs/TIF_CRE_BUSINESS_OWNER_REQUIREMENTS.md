## Primary buyer journeys

### Tenant representation

- New location or market entry.
- Lease renewal versus relocation.
- Expansion, contraction, or consolidation.
- Medical/service operator site selection.
- Corridor and center comparison.
- Buildout, access, parking, visibility, configuration, and timing risk.

Until licensing is active and the brokerage approves the marketing, CTAs route to a confirmed
licensed commercial teammate. After activation, CTA changes require verified license status,
registered brokerage, advertising approval, and explicit representation language.

### Business-owner transferability and exit readiness

- Owner wants the company to operate without constant personal intervention.
- Owner anticipates succession, transfer, recapitalization, or a future sale.
- Management, workflow, reporting, customer concentration, or key-person risk constrains confidence.
- Business needs an evidence-backed operating system before independent transaction advisors can
  evaluate readiness.

TKO may offer an operational-readiness assessment and a scoped $25K+ monthly implementation when
diagnosis supports the scope. Content must never claim that the engagement is required to sell,
calculate a valuation, guarantee a premium, or replace licensed brokerage, legal, tax, accounting,
or financial advice.

## Field media workflow

1. Plan a safe route and shot list.
2. Capture while parked or with a passenger; never operate the camera while driving.
3. Record location, date/time, route, photographer, usage rights, and visible conditions.
4. Upload media to an approved durable store.
5. Add its URL and transcript/voice memo to the TIF Capture Inbox.
6. Promote verified observations into `cre-intelligence` entities or evidence.
7. Generate reports and channel packages with source references.

Direct binary storage is deliberately not implemented in TIF. TIF now accepts the durable media
reference and evidence metadata; a blob provider should be selected only when the future publisher
requires direct upload.

## Activation gates

Before public brokerage CTAs:

- Active Florida license verified.
- Employing/registered brokerage verified.
- Brokerage advertising and brand rules documented.
- Referral and compensation flow reviewed.
- Commercial teammate and service boundary approved.
- Privacy, fair-housing, record-retention, and platform review completed.
- Business-brokerage language reviewed with qualified Florida counsel/brokerage leadership.

Before $25K+ operating-system positioning:

- A named diagnostic and deliverable are defined.
- TKO identity, proof, and engagement scope are public and credible.
- Clear separation from valuation and transaction brokerage.
- At least one evidence-backed business-owner case or first-party proof asset.
- No promise of premium, multiple expansion, sale, financing, or time-to-close.
