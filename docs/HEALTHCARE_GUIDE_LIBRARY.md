## Deferred guides

Claims intake/adjudication, eligibility, benefits, and funding are **P2**. The library may collect questions and direct sources, but must not publish educational authority or conversion assets for those domains yet.

## LinkedIn package standard

One package contains: a 250–400 word executive post, a 6–8 slide carousel outline tied to the diagram, three opening hooks, and a single assessment CTA. It must use a pattern observation rather than an unverified client story.
