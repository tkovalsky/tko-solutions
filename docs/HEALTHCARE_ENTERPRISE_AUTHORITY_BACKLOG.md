# Healthcare Enterprise Authority Backlog

**Purpose:** the claim-bounded operating-knowledge plan for healthcare authority. This is not an article calendar and it does not authorize healthcare product claims.

**Validated inputs reused:** `content/proof/healthcare/evidence.yaml`, `content/proof/healthcare/HEALTHCARE_EXPERIENCE_LIBRARY.md`, `docs/PROBLEM_LIBRARY.md`, `docs/HEALTHCARE_AUTHORITY_ARTIFACT_PROGRAM.md`, `docs/TIF_KNOWLEDGE_DIAGRAM_BACKLOG.md`, the existing Prior Authorization Operational Assessment, and the Operational Recovery Assessment.

## Evidence and priority rules

- **P0 — publish now:** an existing healthcare evidence record or verified reusable operating artifact supports a bounded, advisory or illustrative asset. No measured result, named organization, patient detail, or regulatory-compliance claim.
- **P1 — create the proof artifact first:** experience supports the operating hypothesis, but the library needs a reviewed composite, assessment output, or authorized source before a flagship public asset.
- **P2 — research or engagement capture only:** no direct evidence supports an authority claim. Keep the domain as a discovery question; do not publish a guide, proposal promise, or SEO landing page.
- Every public asset inherits the narrowest source claim guard. The standing boundary is: *advisory experience or illustrative operating model; not a client case study, product proof, measured outcome, legal opinion, clinical guidance, or compliance certification.*
