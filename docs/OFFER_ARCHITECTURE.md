# Offer Architecture: Human API Risk Assessment

## Offer Principles

The offer set moves from diagnosis to operating truth to decision-layer design to ongoing advisory. Every offer is documentation, advisory, and operating model work unless implementation is separately scoped later.

Pricing ranges are planning assumptions and require validation through sales conversations.

## 1. Human API Recovery Assessment

### Audience

Healthcare operations and transformation leaders who suspect that critical work depends on a few overloaded individuals.

Best-fit buyers:

- VP Operations
- VP Utilization Management
- VP Care Management
- Chief Transformation Officer
- COO
- CIO

### Core Problem

Work slows, decisions stall, and operational risk rises when a small number of people carry memory, routing, escalation, and exception logic for critical workflows.

### Deliverables

- Human API inventory
- Workflow dependency map
- Knowledge concentration analysis
- Bottleneck and escalation analysis
- Human API Risk Score by workflow
- AI readiness and governance readiness assessment
- Top remediation opportunities
- 90-day action plan
- Executive readout

### Timeline

3 to 4 weeks.

### Pricing Range

$25,000 to $45,000.

### Expected Outcomes

- Leadership can see where operational dependency risk lives.
- Critical workflows are prioritized for remediation.
- Hidden human routing and shadow processes become explicit.
- A follow-on decision layer, operating truth, or advisory engagement is clearly scoped.

## 2. Operational Truth Diagnostic

### Audience

Organizations facing stalled transformation, disputed metrics, unclear workflow reality, or executive uncertainty about what is actually happening.

Best-fit buyers:

- Transformation Office leaders
- Program and portfolio leaders
- Operations executives
- Consulting partners

### Core Problem

The organization has reports, meetings, and status updates, but no trusted view of operational reality.

### Deliverables

- Current-state workflow truth map
- Formal process versus actual process comparison
- Decision and escalation inventory
- Reporting and meeting dependency analysis
- Visibility gap map
- Risk and recovery narrative
- Prioritized recovery roadmap
- Executive readout

### Timeline

3 weeks.

### Pricing Range

$20,000 to $35,000.

### Expected Outcomes

- Sponsor understands where the operating model is breaking down.
- Reporting is separated from operational truth.
- Recovery actions are sequenced around real constraints.

## 3. Decision Layer Build Sprint

### Audience

Teams that have identified Human API dependency and need to design the missing decision layer before automation or AI implementation.

Best-fit buyers:

- Operations leaders
- Product and transformation leaders
- Health tech delivery teams
- AI program owners

### Core Problem

The workflow cannot be safely modernized because decision criteria, required facts, exception handling, approval authority, and escalation logic are not explicit.

### Deliverables

- Decision inventory and authority model
- Fact requirements by decision
- Exception and escalation rules
- Human approval model
- Canonical next-action logic design
- Governance cadence
- Measurement model
- Implementation-ready operating model documentation

### Timeline

4 to 6 weeks.

### Pricing Range

$40,000 to $75,000.

### Expected Outcomes

- The organization has a clear decision layer that can guide workflow redesign, AI support, or software implementation.
- Human approval points are explicit.
- Automation prerequisites are defined.
- Implementation teams receive a coherent operating model instead of loose requirements.

## 4. Fractional Operational Intelligence Advisor

### Audience

Organizations that need senior operating model guidance after an assessment, diagnostic, or decision-layer sprint.

Best-fit buyers:

- COO
- CIO
- Chief Transformation Officer
- VP Operations
- Consulting partner managing a complex client engagement

### Core Problem

Assessment findings lose momentum without an operating cadence, executive translation, governance discipline, and remediation oversight.

### Deliverables

- Weekly or biweekly advisory cadence
- Remediation backlog governance
- Executive decision support
- Human API dependency reduction tracking
- AI and automation readiness review
- Cross-functional risk surfacing
- Sponsor readout support
- Monthly operating intelligence memo

### Timeline

3 to 6 months.

### Pricing Range

$15,000 to $30,000 per month.

### Expected Outcomes

- Recovery work stays visible and governed.
- Dependency reduction becomes part of the operating cadence.
- Leaders get a consistent translation layer between operational reality and executive decisions.

## Offer Ladder

Recommended path:

1. Human API Recovery Assessment
2. Decision Layer Build Sprint
3. Fractional Operational Intelligence Advisor

Alternate path for stalled programs:

1. Operational Truth Diagnostic
2. Human API Recovery Assessment for the riskiest workflow
3. Decision Layer Build Sprint

Partner path:

1. Operational Truth Diagnostic embedded in consulting engagement
2. Decision Layer Build Sprint supporting implementation team
3. Fractional advisory for governance and adoption
