# Healthcare Enterprise Authority Library

The Healthcare Enterprise Authority Library is the operating-knowledge system for TKO's healthcare authority program. Its purpose is to make enterprise healthcare judgment inspectable through publishable, claim-bounded operating artifacts — not to produce generic healthcare articles or imply product, clinical, legal, regulatory, or outcome credentials that the evidence does not support.

## Library map

| Library | Role |
|---|---|
| [Healthcare Authority Backlog](./HEALTHCARE_ENTERPRISE_AUTHORITY_BACKLOG.md) | Canonical domain records: executive problems, operating context, assets, evidence, claim boundary, and P0/P1/P2 sequence. |
| [Healthcare Diagram Backlog](./HEALTHCARE_DIAGRAM_BACKLOG.md) | Production order and evidence gate for every healthcare knowledge diagram. |

| [Healthcare SEO Roadmap](./HEALTHCARE_SEO_ROADMAP.md) | Evidence-first search clusters and indexability gates. |

## What was validated and reused

- The Healthcare Experience Library and evidence registry establish advisory experience for prior authorization, utilization management, care management, interoperability, regulatory initiatives, workflow modernization, and transformation governance.
- The existing Prior Authorization Operational Assessment, its two published insights, and the current/future-state PA diagrams are the immediate P0 conversion foundation.
- The Operational Recovery Assessment, Executive Operating Review, dependency/decision artifacts, and Problem Library are the enterprise program-recovery foundation.
- RachelOS provides verified implementation proof for source authority, human approval, operating memory, priority, and observability. It is kept distinct from healthcare advisory experience.

## Priority contract

| Priority | Meaning | Publication rule |
|---|---|---|
| P0 | Evidence or a verified reusable artifact exists now. | Publish an advisory or illustrative operating artifact with its source and claim boundary. |
| P1 | The operating pattern is credible, but the proof artifact is incomplete. | Create and review a composite, assessment output, or authorized source before publishing a flagship asset. |
| P2 | No direct evidence supports authority. | Research and capture during future work only; do not publish conversion, SEO, guide, or proposal claims. |

## Standing healthcare claim boundary

> This artifact is based on advisory experience and/or an illustrative operating model. It does not disclose a client workflow, patient information, payer rule, measured outcome, compliance certification, legal interpretation, clinical guidance, or deployed healthcare product.

## Operating workflow

```text
Evidence / experience record
  → executive problem record
  → framework + assessment component
  → reviewed diagram / executive artifact
  → guide + LinkedIn package + proposal module
  → publication and measurement
```

The next production move is the P0A diagram set in the Diagram Backlog, followed by the existing Prior Authorization and Operational Recovery assessment packages. P1 artifacts are promoted only after their evidence gates are met; P2 remains intentionally unbuilt.
