## Editorial Guardrails

The 90-day calendar prioritizes long-tail executive intent from Directors, VPs, CIOs, COOs, Transformation leaders, Operations leaders, and PMO leaders.

Healthcare examples should dominate. Financial Services, Wealth Management, and Capital Markets examples should support the broader Operational Intelligence category.

Do not create generic AI content. Do not publish AI trends, tool roundups, prompt engineering, or ChatGPT tutorials.

Primary CTA for all articles: Start the Diagnostic, routed through the Diagnostic page or diagnostic intake depending on funnel stage.

## Suggested 90-Day Sequence

### Days 1-30: Core Problem Recognition

Publish concepts 1-8 first. These establish the core visibility failure, transformation recovery problem, governance gap, and healthcare administrative burden context.

### Days 31-60: Method And Category Education

Publish concepts 9-17. These explain Operational Intelligence, decision systems, operational truth, and human-controlled AI through executive pain.

### Days 61-90: Conversion And Secondary Pattern Support

Publish concepts 18-25. These move toward diagnostic readiness, health-tech operations, Financial Services support examples, and Operating System Build relevance.

## Internal Linking Pattern

Each article should link to:

- Diagnostic page as primary conversion path.
- One relevant case study when available.
- About page when credibility or cross-industry pattern recognition matters.
- Service ladder only when the article compares Diagnostic, Build, and Fractional paths.

Do not link to empty future industry pages.
