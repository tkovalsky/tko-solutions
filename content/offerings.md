# TKO Offerings

Last updated: 2026-06-15

These offerings are limited to capabilities that exist now or are within 1-2 months of implementation from current code.

## 1. Operational Intelligence Diagnostic

Problem: Leaders cannot see where work stalls, why transformation is not producing value, or what AI should actually automate.

Target customer: Healthcare operations, payer/provider transformation teams, health-tech COOs, complex service businesses.

Deliverables:

- Current-state workflow map.
- "Supposed to happen vs actually happens" assessment.
- Failure-point and manual-handoff inventory.
- Visibility and measurement gap map.
- AI opportunity/control model.
- Top 10 recovery opportunities ranked by value, risk, and effort.
- 90-day execution roadmap.
- Executive readout.

Estimated value: High. This is the clearest first $20k-$35k fixed-fee offer.

Implementation readiness: HIGH.

## 2. Relationship Intelligence System

Problem: Teams lose context across calls, emails, notes, forms, handoffs, and side channels.

Target customer: Healthcare care management, patient access, client success, relationship-driven sales, real estate teams.

Deliverables:

- Relationship update capture.
- Structured fact extraction.
- Source authority model.
- Journey/relationship state model.
- Intelligence-gap questions.
- Operator review workflow.
- Next-action logic and rationale.

Estimated value: High where relationships are high-value or regulated.

Implementation readiness: HIGH for custom implementation.

## 3. Lead Recovery and Follow-Up Intelligence

Problem: Leads or cases enter the system but follow-up is inconsistent, stale, or invisible.

Target customer: Real estate teams, healthcare growth teams, patient acquisition, B2B services.

Deliverables:

- Lead leakage audit.
- Canonical next-action queue.
- Freshness and neglect detection.
- Daily action digest.
- Action logging.
- Alerts for silent failure.
- Follow-up QA process.

Estimated value: High because it recovers existing demand.

Implementation readiness: HIGH.

## 4. Human-Controlled AI Workflow Automation

Problem: Organizations want AI leverage but cannot allow uncontrolled AI actions.

Target customer: Regulated operations, healthcare, financial services, enterprise support, sales ops.

Deliverables:

- AI extraction workflow.
- AI draft workflow.
- Human approval gate.
- Audit trail.
- Copy/content safety checks.
- Deterministic decision layer.
- Rollout and governance playbook.

Estimated value: High because it enables AI adoption with operational control.

Implementation readiness: HIGH. Rachel has working patterns for content and outreach approval.

## 5. Reporting Workflow Modernization

Problem: Reports are manually produced, inconsistent, stale, or not useful for decisions.

Target customer: Healthcare operations, CRE/real estate operators, service businesses with recurring executive reports.

Deliverables:

- Report inventory.
- Executive summary template.
- Source/confidence model.
- QA checklist.
- Manual-to-assisted workflow.
- Optional markdown/report publishing pipeline.

Estimated value: Medium-high.

Implementation readiness: MEDIUM. Templates exist, but automated report generation is not proven in this repo.

## 6. Referral and Exception Routing

Problem: Out-of-scope leads, patients, cases, or opportunities get stuck because the main workflow is not built for exceptions.

Target customer: Healthcare referral ops, multi-location services, real estate teams, specialist networks.

Deliverables:

- Exception taxonomy.
- Partner-needed flagging.
- Referral queue.
- Referral action rationale.
- Close-loop states and suppression rules.
- Digest/operator surfacing.

Estimated value: Medium.

Implementation readiness: MEDIUM. Initial referral intelligence exists; full referral lifecycle should be completed before selling as a standalone implementation.

## Do Not Sell Yet

- Multi-tenant product.
- Intelligence graph.
- Autonomous outreach.
- Automated report engine.
- CRM integration package.
- Model gateway.
- Full content strategy engine.
