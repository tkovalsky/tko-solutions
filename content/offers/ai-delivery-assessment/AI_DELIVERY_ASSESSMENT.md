## The offer

A fixed-scope evidence audit for leaders evaluating AI-assisted delivery or an existing "built with AI" system. The method is the one demonstrated publicly on RachelOS:

1. **Evidence hierarchy:** implementation > production configuration > production records > documentation.
2. **Built / Activated / Validated map** of the client's tools, workflows, and AI usage (four-status honesty scale).
3. **Ranked constraint list** with evidence per ranking (the format that surfaced RachelOS's never-touched-lead cohort and 2.2% email-first reply rate).
4. **Governance findings:** approval points, activation gates, audit trails, knowledge concentration.
5. **Executive briefing:** stop / deepen / build recommendation.
