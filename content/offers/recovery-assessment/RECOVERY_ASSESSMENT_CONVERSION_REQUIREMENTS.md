# Operational Recovery Assessment Conversion Requirements

**Purpose:** Define the conversion path for the Operational Recovery Assessment without
building software. This supports the public landing page, intake, discovery workflow,
one-page PDF, and follow-up sequence.

**Authority:** [`CURRENT_REALITY.md`](../../../CURRENT_REALITY.md)

**Primary CTA:** Schedule an Operational Recovery Assessment

**Secondary CTA:** Schedule an Operational Truth Diagnostic

---

## Landing Page Requirements

The public page at `/services/recovery-assessment` must communicate:

1. The buyer has a workflow under pressure.
2. Critical knowledge, decisions, and context may live inside people instead of systems.
3. Healthcare workflow modernization is the primary market wedge.
4. The assessment is fixed-scope: 1 week, $5K-$8K.
5. Deliverables are concrete:
   - Workflow map
   - Bottleneck analysis
   - Dependency analysis
   - AI readiness assessment
   - Operational risk assessment
   - Executive briefing
6. RachelOS is visible proof that TKO can build governed operational systems.
7. The next step is a qualification intake, not an automated purchase.

---

## Intake Form Requirements

The intake should remain lightweight and qualification-oriented.

**Required fields:**
- Name
- Work email
- Company
- Role/title
- Workflow or operating process under pressure
- Systems/artifacts involved
- Where work stalls
- Who currently acts as the human API
- Whether AI or automation is already involved
- Decision leadership needs to make next

**Qualification tags:**
- Healthcare fit
- Health-tech fit
- Consulting partner fit
- Operationally broken service business
- Diagnostic candidate
- Nurture
- Disqualify

**Hard disqualifiers:**
- Commodity automation request
- Model-selection-only request
- SaaS/platform shopping
- Staff augmentation request
- No concrete workflow or operating problem

---

## Discovery Workflow

1. Intake received.
2. Founder reviews the trigger, role, workflow, and fit.
3. If fit is plausible, founder sends a personal response with a 30-minute scheduling link.
4. Discovery call qualifies:
   - active workflow pressure
   - sponsor authority
   - access to workflow evidence
   - whether one week is enough
   - whether deeper Diagnostic is more appropriate
5. If Assessment fits, send fixed-scope assessment summary, price range, start date, and
   invoice terms.
6. If Diagnostic fits better, route to Offer 1.
7. If no fit, politely decline or refer out.

No fully automated sales sequence should send without founder approval.

## One-Page PDF Requirements

The one-page PDF should function as a leave-behind after referral conversations and
discovery calls.

**Required sections:**
- Headline: Operational Recovery Assessment
- Problem: critical workflow knowledge lives inside people instead of systems
- Primary market: healthcare workflow modernization
- Who it is for
- What is included
- Timeline and price: 1 week, $5K-$8K
- Deliverables
- What leadership gets at the end
- RachelOS proof strip: deployed, runs daily, human-approved
- CTA: Schedule an Operational Recovery Assessment

**Constraints:**
- No fabricated metrics.
- No client names.
- No exaggerated AI claims.
- Healthcare proof uses experience and operational lessons unless verified outcomes are
  separately approved.

---

## Follow-Up Sequence Requirements

All follow-up is human-approved.

- **T+0 automatic acknowledgment:** receipt confirmation and what happens next.
- **T+1 founder response:** personalized response referencing the stated workflow trigger.
- **T+3 nudge:** one relevant proof asset such as RachelOS, prior authorization story, or
  transformation recovery story.
- **T+7 final touch:** close the loop for now and move to nurture if no response.
- **Post-discovery:** send assessment scope or diagnostic recommendation within 24 hours.

Every active lead must have one next action and one due date.
